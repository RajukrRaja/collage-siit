VISSION & MISSION

VISSION

Vision To emerge as a group of institutions which not only imbibes new ideas and knowledge among talented young minds but also sensitizes them towards social responsibilities they have as young citizens of India. Further to this, Satyam International endeavours for total quality management.

The institutions will strive to achieve this vision by:

1. A team of dedicated and competent staff in excellence-oriented learning environment.

2. Mutually beneficial and Constructive interaction with corporates to develop industry-institutions relationship

3. Strengthening community services by developing young human resource.

4. Inculcating the qualities of leadership, skills and values by infusing inquisitiveness and time-honoured virtues in the students

MISSION

Our mission is to nurture the enormous talent of the students by providing them inspiring ambience in which students feel highly motivated to strive in a diligent and dedicated manner. They will be equipped with the skills and strengths that they need to cope with the challenges that life has in store for them.

we are committed to:

1. Offering best quality education to students

2. Producing skilled manpower meeting international standards and global requirements

3. Ensuring optimum utilization of available resources

4. Providing transparent and efficient administration

5. Conducting job-oriented courses in appropriate technologies for the benefits of society.

6.Interacting with industries, alumni and society by offering mutually beneficial services, creating unshakable trust and maintaining high moral standards.
